### Commands
**Admin**:
* Player need permission: plex2.admin
* Give player gems: /gem <player> <amount> 
* Give player shards: /shard <player> <amount>
* Set the spawn: /setspawn
* Reload plex: /plexreload

**Default commands**:
* Teleport to hub: /hub
